What is binary?

Computers are not very clever things. All they can understand are '1' and '0' which you would think would be very limiting.

Every single thing you can see on your screen right now is made up of these 1s and 0s.

Your computer (and everything else electronic!) converts everything to this language so that it can understand what it needs to do.
